## üí¨ Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Will I hear the original Minecraft music?** A: No. This mod completely overrides the vanilla music engine to ensure only your custom tracks play.

**Q: Can I change the volume while a song is playing?** A: Yes! The volume buttons update the audio levels in real-time.

**Q: What file formats are supported?** A: Only .mp3 files are supported thus far, I am unfortunately not planning to add compat for other formats.
